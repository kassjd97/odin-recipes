Rajas con queso

Home page

Rajas con queso is a delicious dish that brings up many flavours using unexpeced ingredients. Have you ever thought about mixing cream and chili? I know, it sounds crazy! But it turns to be the best flavour ever!

Ingredients

  • 4 Poblano pepers
  • 1/2 cup of manchego cheese
  • 1/2 cup of sour cream
  • 1 whole onion in half-moon slices
  • The kernels of 1 whole corn
  • 1 spn of oil
  • 1 spn of butter
  • 2 tspn of chicken bouillon
  • 1 tspn of salt

Steps

  1. Roast the peppers rigt into the flame until the peel gets completely burnt.
  2. Place the peppers into a plastic bag and let them chill until room temperature.
  3. Take the pepers out of the bag, peel them off and cut them into juliannes.
  4. Boil the kernels with salt until they get soft, about 10 min. and then drain the water off.
  5. Medium heat a sauce pan with the oil and butter and cook with the onion until this one turns transparent
  6. Add the peppers and kernels, then add the chicken bouillon and cook for about 3 minutes.
  7. Turn the heat to low and add the cheese and sour cream, turn off the heat once the cheese gets melted
