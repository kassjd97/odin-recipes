Jericalla

Jericalla is a sweet and unique dessert, and guess what? It's super easy to make!

Ingredients

  • Whole milk
  • Evaporated milk
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon stick
  • Vanilla extract
  • Large eggs (Room temperature)
  • Egg yolk
  • Water

Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan, heat up the milks, sugar, cinnamon and vanilla over medium heat.
  2. After 5 minutes, turn the heat down to low heat for an extra 15 minutes.
  3. Strain the mixture and set aside to cool completely.
  4. Meanwhile, beat the eggs.
  5. Mix little by little the eggs with the milk mixture in a large bowl
  6. Do this until the eggs are fully incorporated

    7. WARNING! Be sure the milk is cool. You do not want to cook the eggs, or you'll need to start over.

  7. To set up the water bath, place individual 8oz porcelain ramekins inside a large baking dish or roasting pan.
  8. Pour the egg-milk mixture into containers.
